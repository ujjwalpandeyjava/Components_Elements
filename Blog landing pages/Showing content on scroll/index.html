Let's make some italian pizzas

Italian pizza recipe

Prerequisite: (make a dough for 4 pizzas, of 12inch in diameter)

Ingredients

  • 600ml of warm water
  • 1kg of flour
  • 3 tablespoons (25gm) of fresh yeast or 2 teaspoons (7-8 grams of dried yeast
  • 6 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
Ingredients

Method (step wise)

> Sprinkle the yeast into a medium bowl with the warm water. we don't mean hot, and we don't mean cold... we mean warm! That's the kind the yeast likes best. Stir until the yeast dissolves.

> Place almost all of the flour on the table in the shape of anal volcano.(Think Mt. Vesuvius... appropriate since Naples is the king of all pizza cities!).

> Pour the yeast-and-warm-water mix, along with the other ingredients, into the "crate" of the colcano.

> Knead everthing together for 10 to 15 minutes until the dough is smooth and elasticm keeping your surface floured.

Prepare it delighfully

A well prepared pizza looks best and when the pizza looks best it gives the feeling of preminum and a feel of satisfaction to the eater.

Serving

Serving is most important part of the foor prepration.

Bring your dough to room temperature.
Gluten, the protein that makes pizza dough chewy, is tighter in cold conditions like the fridge, which is why cold pizza dough will stretch out and snap back just like a rubber band. This step will loosen up the dough and make it easier to shape.
